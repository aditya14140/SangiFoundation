About Sangi Foundation

Sangi Foundation is a dedicated non-profit organization committed to creating positive social impact through community development, education, and sustainable programs. Since our inception, we've been working tirelessly to empower marginalized communities and provide opportunities for growth and development.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower underprivileged communities through education, healthcare, and sustainable livelihood programs, fostering self-reliance and dignity for all individuals.

Our Vision

A world where every individual has access to basic necessities, quality education, and opportunities to thrive, regardless of their socio-economic background.

Our Story

Founded with a vision to bridge the gap between privilege and poverty, Sangi Foundation began its journey with a small group of dedicated volunteers who believed in the power of collective action. Over the years, we have grown into a robust organization, touching thousands of lives across multiple communities.

Our approach is rooted in collaboration, sustainability, and dignity. We work closely with local communities to understand their needs and develop programs that create lasting impact. Every initiative we undertake is designed to empower individuals and communities to become self-reliant.

What We Do

Educational Programs

We run scholarship programs, after-school tutoring, digital literacy classes, and provide educational materials to students in need.

Healthcare Initiatives

Mobile health clinics, health camps, vaccination drives, and awareness programs on nutrition and hygiene.

Skill Development

Vocational training, entrepreneurship workshops, and job placement assistance for youth and women.

Community Development

Infrastructure development, clean water projects, and sustainable agriculture programs in rural areas.
